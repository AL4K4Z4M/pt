Our Mission

At PlateTraits, our mission is to create a community-driven platform for anonymous, lighthearted feedback on driving behavior. We believe that a little bit of feedback can go a long way in making the roads a more pleasant and predictable place for everyone. We're not about shaming, we're about sharing quick, simple, and constructive observations.

What We're Building

PlateTraits is a digital space where you can leave a note about a license plate. Did someone let you merge with a friendly wave? Give them a "Courteous" trait. Did someone cut you off? A "Needs Improvement" might be in order. The idea is to keep it simple, safe, and respectful.

We are focused on:

  • Safety: Our service is designed to be used *after* your drive is complete. Never use PlateTraits while operating a vehicle.
  • Simplicity: Pre-set traits and simple forms make leaving feedback quick and easy.

The Team

We're a small, independent team of developers and driving enthusiasts who thought, "What if there was a Yelp for license plates?" This project is a labor of love, and we're excited to see where the community takes it. We're constantly working on new features and improvements.
